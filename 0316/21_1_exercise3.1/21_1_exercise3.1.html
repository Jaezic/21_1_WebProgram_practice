Computer software (often called just software) is made of one or more
computer programs. Sometimes it means one specific program, or it can
mean all the software on a computer, Including the applications and the
operating system.
Applications are programs that do a specific thing. Such as a game or a word
processor. The operating system (Windows 7, OS X, Linux, etc.) is a software
that helps the applications run, and controls the display and the keyboard.
